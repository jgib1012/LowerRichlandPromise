Building a Promise for Lower Richland

Lower Richland Promise is a community-based nonprofit focused on food access, education, and community empowerment for youth, families, and seniors in the Lower Richland area.

This website is a digital hub where neighbors, partners, and donors can learn about our work, see our impact, and find ways to get involved.
