Our Programs

Lower Richland Promise coordinates programs that focus on food access, education, and community empowerment. Below are example program areas that can be refined as the organization grows.

Food Distribution Image Placeholder

Food Access & Distribution

Regular food distribution events and partnerships that help families access fresh, healthy food in the Lower Richland area.

Youth Program Image Placeholder

Youth Enrichment & Education

After-school support, enrichment activities, and mentorship opportunities that help young people grow academically, socially, and emotionally.

Senior Support Image Placeholder

Senior & Family Support

Programs and events that support seniors and caregivers, helping them stay connected and supported within the community.

Community Events Image Placeholder

Community Events & Outreach

Community gatherings, resource fairs, and special events that bring residents together and connect them with services and opportunities.
